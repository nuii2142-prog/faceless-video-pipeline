I haven't used an alarm clock in three years. The vegetables wake me up. Most people think waking up early is a willpower game. On a farm, the plants set the schedule. Water them too late. You lose them. This morning I walked out before the sun came up. The soil was still cool under my bare feet. I could smell the wet earth before I could see the rows. My hands started working before my mind was awake. Find something outside yourself that needs you at a specific time. It works better than discipline or any alarm clock.
